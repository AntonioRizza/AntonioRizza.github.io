Antonio Rizza

Antonio Rizza is a Postdoctoral Researcher at Stanford University in the Space Rendezvous Laboratory working on autonomous Guidance Navigation and Control for Distributed Space Systems. He has a PhD in aerospace engineering from Politecnico di Milano where he focused on autonomous goal-oriented guidance for small bodies proximity operations and developed GNC solutions for the Milani and LUMIO CubeSats.
